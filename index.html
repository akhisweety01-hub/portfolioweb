Aadhaar Pulse — Predictive Biometric Service Framework

A full AI-powered decision-support engine that transforms UIDAI's Aadhaar service delivery from a reactive "walk-in" model to a proactive, data-driven "reach-out" infrastructure — targeting 100% biometric compliance through intelligent resource allocation across 1.4 billion identities.

The Problem

170 million children have pending mandatory biometric updates (MBU). Districts like Gaya (Bihar) and Pune register massive demand while centers stay static — creating "Aadhaar Deserts" where rural zones lack any center within a 10km radius. High-demand pincodes often have the lowest service density, causing exclusion from school admissions, scholarships, and DBT schemes.

Proprietary Metric — District Stress Index (DSI)
DSI = (MBU_Debt × 0.45) + (Load_Current × 0.35) + (Gap_Equip × 0.20)
Future MBU Debt — 45% Transaction Velocity — 35% Equipment Gap — 20%
